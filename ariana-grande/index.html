    ARIANA GRANDE STARS IN NEW T-MOBILE COMMERCIAL WITH HER SONG 'SIDE TO SIDE'

    On October 10th, Ariana Grande has lent her image for a new T-Mobile commercial. The 'Side to Side' star is seen being the co-pilot in a car with a girl fan who's traveling across the desert. It all will be nice between her until a Verizon data alert message pops up on the fan's phone. She will then be forced to literally unload Ariana off of her car and stop playing her music so she can use her remaining plan data for her GPS/Google Maps only.

  • 25 SEP 2016

    ARIANA GRANDE PERFORMS AT 2016 IHEARTRADIO MUSIC FESTIVAL

    On September 24th, Ariana Grande's performance was one of the highlights of night two of the 2016 iHeartRadio Music Festival.

    Grande was among those selected to headline on Saturday night, along with Britney Spears, Sting, Pitbull, and more. Her set began with 'Into You'. She then went into 'Be Alright', followed by 'Everyday'. Next, Grande brought Zedd onstage to play the drums while she performed 'Break Free'. She then sang 'Side To Side' and 'Dangerous Woman'.
